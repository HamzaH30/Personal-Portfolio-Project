This program uses Selenium Web Driver to automatically do an internet speed test, then, if the internet speed is very bad, log into Twitter to tweet/complain about why the user's internet is so bad.

Stock News Alert Program

This project uses APIs to send a SMS to a user when a stock increases/decreases by (for example) 5%. The program will also send 3 news articles related to the stock at the time of the stock price change.

Science Fairs

Best in Computer Science Project - Blockchain & Pharmaceutical

Detecting Counterfeit Medicine in the Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Using Blockchain Technology

In grade 8, I had participated in the Acadia Junior High science fair. This time I was researching the topic of blockchain technology. I was also reading about how an increase of counterfeit medicines had caused a lot of people in underdeveloped areas serious illnesses and in most cases, death. I wanted to develop a model of using blockchain technology and integrate it into the pharmaceutical industry. I participated in the Acadia Junior High science fair and won the Best in Computer Science award and a gold medal.

Best in Grade Science Fair

The Effects of Financial News on Investor(s) Behaviour in The Stock Market

In grade 7, I once again participated in the science fair. This time I decided to do an independent project. My science fair was titled "The Effects of Financial News on Investor Behaviour in the Stock Market". I had worked on my science fair project for more than 4 months! In this science fair project, I improved my research, presentation, and report-writing skills. I used proper terminology in different parts of my report which my judges commended. For my test subjects, I had various people from different age groups and experience in the stock market participate in my experiment. In the Acadia Junior High science fair, I won a gold medal and a Best in Grade award. In the Manitoba Schools Science Symposium (MSSS) I won a silver medal.

Best Overall Group Project

In 2018, my friend and I had been working on a science fair project for about 3 months. Our science fair project was called: "Does temperature affect the bounce of a tennis ball?" We had conducted multiple experiments during different conditions to see the result and test our hypothesis. In the Manitoba Schools Science Symposium (MSSS) we had won a gold medal, Best Overall Group Project, and a special award from Engineers Geoscientists Manitoba. This was a very interesting experience for me and helped spark my interest in science and science fairs.
